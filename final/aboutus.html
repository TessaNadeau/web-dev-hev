Coffee Location About Us

Coffea works in the development of new techniques together with the producers in the processing of coffee to obtain a better cup profile. This allows the development of the coffee plantations and to be able to sell the coffee at a better price, making this a sustainable process for the coffee farmer and the cafeteria.

In addition to amazing coffee, you can find Guatemalan artisan chocolate and tea imported from Taiwan. Get out of the routine, enjoy a great cup of coffee and have a good time among people who love what they do.
