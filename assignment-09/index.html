1920's Fashion

Paris set the fashion trends for Europe and North America.
The fashion for women was all about getting loose.
Women wore dresses all day, everyday.
Day dresses had a drop waist, which was a sash or belt around
the low waist or hip and a skirt that hung anywhere
from the ankle on up to the knee, never above.
Daywear had sleeves (long to mid-bicep) and a skirt that was,
straight, pleaded, hank hem, or tired. Jewelry was less
conspicuous.Hair was often bobbed, giving a boyish look.
